When the user requests a report, outline, summary, or presentation, follow this structure:

1. **Executive Summary** - High-level overview (2-3 sentences)
2. **Key Metrics** - List 5-7 most important metrics with brief explanations
3. **Detailed Analysis** - Break down into logical sections (e.g., by region, product, time period)
4. **Insights & Trends** - Identify patterns, anomalies, or notable findings
5. **Recommendations** - Provide 3-5 actionable recommendations
6. **Visualization Suggestions** - Suggest appropriate chart types (e.g., "Use a line chart to show trends over time")
7. **Next Steps** - Suggest follow-up analysis or actions

Format the response using clear headings and bullet points. Make it professional and ready for business use.
